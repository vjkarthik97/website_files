Motivation

Mobile Robots are a class of underactuated systems with inherent non-holonomic constraints that restrict their maneuverability. In practice, they are often deployed in space-constrained operating workspaces. Therefore, motion planning for such systems becomes a complex task, and it is significantly challenging when the exact obstacle configuration is not known beforehand.
